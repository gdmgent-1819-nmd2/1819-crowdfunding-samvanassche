ABOUT

OUR STORY

SOLOSOCKS was established in Copenhagen in 2016 based on the problem of missing socks. Coming from big families, our founders were tired of pairing socks during each laundry session. Why was it that socks kept on going missing? After a ton of research, we realized that there was no explanation. Socks simply just disappear.
Instead of finding out what the problem was, we decided to come up with a solution: SOLOSOCKS - a pack of 7 single socks designed to mix and match. This meant that you never had to spend time pairing your socks again. When you have SOLOSOCKS, you simply take the socks that look similar and put them together in the packaging which turns into a portable storage box.

SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

SOLOSOCKS

At SOLOSOCKS we are conscious about our economic, social and environmental responsibilities.
We strive to deliver Danish design, combined with innovation and high quality. We seek to make your everyday life simple and easy with a social and environmental responsibility in mind. Our suppliers and vendors comply with all the necessary European norms and standards within their respective areas.
When selecting vendors and materials we will always prioritize organic materials and place sustainability high on our priority list.
We use only organic combed cotton for our SOLOSOCKS™. The cotton is purchased from a GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certified producer.
The storage box and packaging materials are made of recyclable cardboard produced by the manufacturer ‘Nilorn’ who has signed the ‘UN Global Compacts principles of sustainability’.
